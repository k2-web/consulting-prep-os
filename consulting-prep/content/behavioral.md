# Behavioral Interview Mastery

**The STAR Method**: The gold standard for answering "Tell me about a time..." questions.

## 1. Situation (10%)

Set the scene briefly.

* "I was leading a team of 5 analysts on a due diligence project..."
* "Our client was facing a 15% revenue decline..."

## 2. Task (10%)

Describe the challenge or objective.

* "My goal was to identify $10M in cost savings..."
* "We needed to deliver the final report in 48 hours..."

## 3. Action (60%)

**This is the most important part.** What did *YOU* do?

* "I built a dynamic financial model..."
* "I facilitated a workshop with key stakeholders..."
* "I mediated the conflict by..."
* *Tip: Use "I" statements, not "We".*

## 4. Result (20%)

Quantify the outcome.

* "As a result, we identified $12M in savings (20% above target)..."
* "The client adopted our recommendation and saw a 5% stock price increase..."

> [!TIP]
> Prepare 5-7 "Power Stories" that can be adapted to different questions (Leadership, Conflict, Failure, Success).
