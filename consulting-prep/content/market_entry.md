# Market Entry Framework

**Core Question**: Should we enter this new market?

## 1. The Market (Attractiveness)

* **Size & Growth**: TAM (Total Addressable Market), SAM, SOM.
* **Profitability**: Margins in this industry.
* **Porter's 5 Forces**:
    1. **Supplier Power**: Can suppliers squeeze us?
    2. **Buyer Power**: Can customers demand lower prices?
    3. **Threat of Substitutes**: Is there a better alternative?
    4. **Threat of New Entrants**: How high are the barriers?
    5. **Competitive Rivalry**: Is it a price war?

## 2. The Client (Capabilities)

* **Financials**: Do we have the cash (Capex)?
* **Synergies**: Can we leverage existing distribution/brand?
* **Expertise**: Do we know this industry?

## 3. Entry Strategy

* **Build (Organic)**: Slow, high control, keep all equity.
* **Buy (Acquisition)**: Fast, expensive, integration risk.
* **Partner (JV)**: Shared risk, local expertise, lower upside.
