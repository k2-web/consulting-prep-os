# PwC Culture & Values

**"Build trust in society and solve important problems."**

## Core Values

1. **Act with Integrity**: Speak up for what is right, especially when it feels difficult.
2. **Make a Difference**: Stay informed and ask questions about the future of the world we live in.
3. **Care**: Make an effort to understand every individual and what matters to them.
4. **Work Together**: Collaborate and share relationships, ideas, and knowledge beyond boundaries.
5. **Reimagine the Possible**: Dare to challenge the status quo and try new things.

## Interview Tips

* **Be Professional**: PwC values a polished, professional demeanor.
* **Be Collaborative**: Show how you work in teams. Use "We" instead of "I" when discussing team projects.
* **Be Curious**: Ask insightful questions about the firm's digital transformation and ESG initiatives.
