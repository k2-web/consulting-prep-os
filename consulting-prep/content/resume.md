# Consulting Resume Checklist

**Goal**: Pass the 6-second screen.

## Formatting

- [ ] **One Page**: Strictly one page. No exceptions.
- [ ] **Font**: Professional (Arial, Calibri, Garamond). Size 10-12.
- [ ] **Margins**: 0.5" to 1.0".
- [ ] **PDF**: Always submit as PDF.

## Content

### Education

- [ ] **GPA**: Include if > 3.5/4.0.
- [ ] **Honors**: Dean's List, Scholarships.
- [ ] **Test Scores**: GMAT/GRE/SAT (optional, but helpful for MBB).

### Experience

- [ ] **Action Verbs**: Start every bullet with a strong verb (Led, Created, Analyzed).
- [ ] **Quantification**: Every bullet must have a number.
  - *Bad*: "Improved sales."
  - *Good*: "Led marketing campaign that increased sales by **20% ($50k)** in Q3."
- [ ] **Result-Oriented**: Show the impact, not just the task.

### Skills & Interests

- [ ] **Hard Skills**: Python, SQL, Excel, PowerPoint.
- [ ] **Interests**: Be specific. "Marathon Runner (3:45 PB)" is better than "Running".
